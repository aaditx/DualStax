FAQ
Common questions
How fast can you deliver?
Most projects are delivered in 7–10 days. Landing pages can be done in as little as 5 days. We'll give you a clear timeline upfront.
How does pricing work?
Every project is custom quoted based on your scope, timeline, and requirements. Reach out and we'll send you a detailed quote within 24 hours. Payment is 50% upfront and 50% on delivery.
Do you offer maintenance after delivery?
Yes — we offer monthly maintenance plans covering hosting, bug fixes, and content updates. Ask us about it when you reach out.
Can I update the website myself?
Business plan and above includes a CMS so you can update content, images, and blog posts without touching code.
What if I'm not happy with the design?
Every plan includes revision rounds. We work with you until you're happy — we won't disappear after delivery.
